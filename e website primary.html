About Us

Welcome to Pattaduru Home Stay – Your Serene Escape into Nature!

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, Pattaduru Home Stay offers a perfect blend of comfort, tranquility, and adventure. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an exciting getaway, our homestay is your gateway to nature’s paradise. Wake up to the melodious chirping of birds, sip on freshly brewed coffee while gazing at misty hills, and immerse yourself in the beauty of unspoiled landscapes. Our cozy accommodations, warm hospitality, and authentic local cuisine ensure a home-away-from-home experience. Explore nearby waterfalls, trek through scenic trails, or simply unwind in our serene surroundings. At Pattaduru Home Stay, every moment is a memory in the making. Come, relax, and reconnect with nature!
